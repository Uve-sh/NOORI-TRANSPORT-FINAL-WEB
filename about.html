Company Profile

Noori Transportation Co. stands as a premier beacon of reliability in the industrial logistics sector, combining over three generations of specialized transport and logistical competence. Founded with a single commercial vehicle, our enterprise has flourished into an multi-faceted logistics operator handling coal mining, bulk cargo transportation, hazardous materials, and energy sector solutions.

We pride ourselves on supporting core industry infrastructure across the nation. Over the decades, our operations have evolved to incorporate advanced fleet safety telemetry, environmental waste compliances, and robust supply chain integrations. Today, Noori Transportation Co. remains a trusted generational partner for chemical industries, mining networks, state corporations, and infrastructure projects.

With an extensive heavy-heavy fleet capacity, including dumpers, liquid tankers, specialized carriers, and earthmoving apparatus, we execute safe transit of commodities under strict compliance with environmental regulations and state safety mandates.

Our Vision & Mission

At Noori Transportation Co., we align our growth with the long-term sustainability of the industries we serve, ensuring national supply chains operate with zero disruptions.

Our Vision

To lead the future of industrial logistics by setting benchmark safety systems, adopting green energy transport modules, and offering unmatched multi-modal supply solutions across India.

Our Mission

To safely, reliably, and efficiently transport industrial commodities while building generational trust with partners, upholding strict regulations, and protecting the environments we navigate.
